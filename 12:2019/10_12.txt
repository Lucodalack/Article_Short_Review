Dynamic programing and Divide and conquer 

Summary:
Dynamic programing is a kind of Divide and conquer, but have some special condition:
Can break in to sub-problem, and sub-problem must be overlaps each other and have optimal substructure
Dynamic programing has 2 way to implement: top-down (memorization) and bottom-up tabulation
